Welcome to the Archive!

This is a collaborative archive. To contribute, click the 'Add New File' button at the top right.

How it works:

  1. Click 'Add New File'.
  2. Fill out the form with the correct Degree, Batch, Year, and Semester for your file.
  3. Select your file and add a descriptive caption.
  4. When you upload, the folders (e.g., your Batch) will be created automatically if they don't already exist.
