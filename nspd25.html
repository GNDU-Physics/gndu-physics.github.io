Mission Directive

Problem: Chandrayaan-4 Lunar Outpost Mandate

Introduction

Following the monumental successes of the Chandrayaan program, the next national objective is the establishment of a semi-permanent research outpost on the Moon. The Chandrayaan-4 mission is the cornerstone of this effort.

Your mission is to analyze multi-layered geospatial data of the lunar south pole and identify the single optimal 1km x 1km grid cell for landing and construction, balancing safety, resources, and power.

Mission Datasets

Analyze these multi-layered geospatial data files.

elevation.csv

A 500x500 matrix of elevation values. Crucial for determining landing site safety.

illumination.csv

Average solar illumination percentage. Key for continuous power generation.

water_ice.csv

Probability of subsurface water-ice deposits. Essential for in-situ resource utilization.

signal_occultation.csv

Annual hours of communication blackout. Vital for mission control link.
